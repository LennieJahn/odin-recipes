Vegan sushi | Easy & Healthy

Super easy and healhy recipes for vegan sushi! This recipe is gluten-free.

Ingredients

  • Tofu and tempeh
  • Ginger and garlic
  • Rice
  • Cucumbers
  • Nori sheets

Steps

  1. Cook the sushi rice

    2. Cook the sushi rice to packet instructions.

  2. Prepare sushi fillings

    3. Cook and/or fry the ingredients to your desired taste.

  3. Roll the sushi

    4. Start with a thin layer of rice, followed by your fillings. Roll the sushi tight and glue the end with some water togehter.

  4. Enjoy
